{{ ritual_title|default:"Moon Blessing" }}

{{ ritual_intent|default:"To bless yourself beneath the moon, gently and completely." }}

You will need: {{ ritual_needs|default:"a clear night, a glass of water, a pinch of dried lavender or rose." }}

The working:

  1. Step outside or stand at an open window. Lift the glass toward the moon.
  2. Speak: "Lady moon, witness me. I am here, I am willing, I am loved."
